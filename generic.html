About the Analyst
Prathyusha Jampala

Highly motivated data analyst with a keen ability to work both independently and collaboratively, always eager to learn and explore new insights to drive data-driven success.

I am a Data Analyst with a strong academic background and diverse experience in data analysis and visualization. I hold a Master’s in Computer Science from the University of North Texas, where I excelled in coursework such as Software Engineering, Big Data & Data Science, and Machine Learning, graduating with a 3.9 GPA. My undergraduate studies in Computer Science and Engineering at Vignan’s Lara Institute of Technology & Sciences laid a solid foundation in data structures, algorithms, and programming languages.

My professional experience includes roles as a Teaching Assistant at UNT and a Junior Data Analyst at Diya Consulting Services. In these positions, I developed and managed analytical reports and dashboards using tools like Tableau, Python, and SQL, contributing to improved decision-making and operational efficiency. I have a proven track record in data cleaning, visualization, and predictive modeling, with hands-on experience in using GCP, AWS, and various machine learning libraries.

Passionate about turning complex data into actionable insights, I continuously seek opportunities to learn and apply advanced analytics techniques. My goal is to leverage my skills and experience to drive data-informed strategies and deliver impactful solutions in a dynamic and collaborative environment.
